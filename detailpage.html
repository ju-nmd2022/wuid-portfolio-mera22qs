COMPANY #2: SWEDISH GIN
In the very north of Sweden,
rich in forests,and unspoiled
nature, two childhood friends
has for many years produced
and sold a locally produced
Gin at selected Systembolaget
stores in northern Sweden.The
Gin is flavoured with the
juniper berries from the
surrounding forests. They have
never had any proper branding
for the bottles though, and the
labels has varied somewhat
throughout the years.Now they
have secured a deal for a larger
launch throughout Sweden and
northern Europe and need your
help coming up with a brand name,
logo and a visual identity.
The brand is not exclusive but
wants to be perceived as clean and
modern but still close-to-nature.
